About Rand

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Southern California, and a research assistant in Laurent Itti's iLab

Research Interests

I am interested in computer vision, specifically in investigating how models of low-level visual processing in humans can be used to enhance machine object recognition performance.

I am also interested in robotics, high-performance distributed computation, and am an all around huge programming nerd. Check out the skills, projects, and publications sections for more info
