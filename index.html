About Rand

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Southern California, and a research assistant in Laurent Itti's iLab

Research Interests

I am also interested in robotics, high-performance distributed computation, and am a huge programming nerd. Check out the skills, projects, and publications sections for more info
